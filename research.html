Cross-Modality Sensing — Wi-Fi & Satellite Integration

Perceptive Mobile AI and Mobile Computing Research Lab, UT Dallas. Independently developing scalable ML pipelines for cross-modal data transfer and inference, integrating Wi-Fi CSI and satellite sensing data streams into a unified representation.

  • Designing pipelines that fuse Wi-Fi Channel State Information (CSI) with satellite-derived sensing data
  • Building on prior WiFi CSI gesture recognition work — per-antenna multichannel processing to preserve discriminative signal
  • Evaluating CNN-based architectures for cross-modal inference at scale

Comparison of VGG-19 & ResNet-50 in Brain Tumor Detection

Published at the 2023 IEEE International Conference for Convergence in Technology (I2CT). Benchmarked two deep learning architectures for brain tumor classification from MRI scans, evaluating tradeoffs in accuracy, training efficiency, and generalization.

  • Comparative evaluation of VGG-19 and ResNet-50 on MRI-based tumor classification
  • Analysis of architecture depth and skip-connection impact on convergence
  • Presented findings at IEEE I2CT 2023
